Blog Section: Technology and Sustainable Living

Exploring Technology and Sustainability

Rochit Tako

Jan 12, 2026 • 6 min read

About Technology

Technology refers to the practical application of scientific knowledge to develop tools, systems, and processes that improve human life and productivity (Kotler, P. and Keller, K.L., 2016). Nowadays, technology has transformed advances in many sectors like communication, healthcare, education, and commerce, which help us to connect in today’s world. Nowadays technology is there to check and solve environmental challenges by supporting renewable energy, sustainable manufacturing, and responsible consumption practices (World Economic Forum, 2021).

Positive Impact of Technology

Technology has played a great role in the positive impact on society by increasing efficiency, reducing costs, and improving quality of life. Digital platforms enable rapid information sharing, helping individuals become more aware of environmental issues and sustainable choices (United Nations, 2023). By the help of technology the use of fossil fuels are being less, In place of using fossil fuels people are using electricity. Technology-driven business models to create green jobs and support sustainable economic growth, contributing to long-term environmental and social benefits (Elkington, 1997).

Negative Impact of Technology

Despite its advantages, technology also has many challenges. Due to the rapid growth of electronic devices, it has increased electronic waste which is making pollution and resource depletion (United Nations, 2023). Due to excessive use of energy from data centers which play a vital role to increase carbon emissions (International Energy Agency, 2022). People are likely to be more reliable on digital technologies which is affecting mental health negatively and reduces face-to-face interaction (Carr, 2010).

How Technology Boosts Sustainable Product Sales

Technology is essential in advancing the market for sustainable products, as it enhances transparency, increases accessibility, and fosters stronger consumer engagement (Kotler, P. and Keller, K.L., 2016). Brands are offering comprehensive product details, certifications, and lifecycle information, supporting consumers in making purchasing goods easy and reliable due to technology (World Economic Forum, 2021).

Sustainability Living Tips and Tricks

Technology has played a powerful role in living a sustainable life easier and more effectively. Digital tools like energy-management applications and smart home devices which help users to monitor and use energy more efficiently, reducing waste particles/materials achieving to live sustainable life (International Energy Agency, 2022).

Interviews with Sustainable Brands & Experts

Technology communicates and engages between sustainable brands, experts, and consumers through different platforms like podcasts, blogs, social media platforms and many more. These platforms collaborate and increase the awareness of how sustainable innovations are suffering through (World Economic Forum, 2021).

News & Updates on Environmental Issues

Nowadays all online news provider and digital media gives us the perfect updates about the climate change according to the time, and environmental policies which helps in improving the information received by the people and supports public engagement (United Nations, 2023). Technology enables data collection and enhances transparency report and accountability in environmental and efforts (International Energy Agency, 2022).

Community Stories & Successes

In the 21st Century many people are sharing their Successes story on their social media which inspire other people to achieve their goals. Stories inspire others to adapt eco-friendly practices and support sustainable products (Kotler, P. and Keller, K.L., 2016). Online crowdfunding platforms and digital campaigns give communities more opportunities to actively support environmental initiatives and social causes (World Economic Forum, 2021).

Conclusion

In 2026, technology is one of the most powerful tools which influence modern society and sustainability efforts. Technology brings many benefits, such as improving efficiency, encouraging innovation, and raising awareness about environmental issues (Carr, 2010). On the other hand, it can create problems like electronic waste, increase in energy consumption, and social inequality (United Nations, 2023). Technology has boosted sustainable product sales, promoted ethical consumption, and supported global sustainability goals.
